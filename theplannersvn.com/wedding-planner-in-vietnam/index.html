Our Wedding
Planning Service

Being recognized for our distinctive and understated style, The Planners has garnered trust from many couples coming from Vietnam and out of its border for infusing their love stories and personality into the wedding.

With years of experience, we understand an incredible wedding goes beyond great decoration; it is about the atmosphere, and most importantly – the emotions experienced by the bride, the groom, and their loved ones. In preparation for it, you will need a detailed plan for every activity before, during and after the big day.

Our step-by-step planningwill soothe your worries, help you to clear your vision and bring your dreams to life. We provide you with our solid knowledge in planning and executing a wedding from start to finish. With a wide network, we curate your venue and vendor team, develop wedding concepts while giving advice and recommendations based on your taste, personality and budget.

With this personal approach,we ensure the wedding that we plan is meaningful and truly reflects our couples as individuals, as couples and state their shared values while sophisticatedly adding style, charm and characteristics to the wedding.

Types of Wedding

Indoor Wedding

Depending on the scale and the concept of the wedding, types of venues for an indoor wedding can vary greatly. From a small intimate wedding party at restaurants to large and lavish weddings at luxurious resorts, we offer different services and ideas to make your day memorable.

Outdoor Wedding

Holding weddings in open space is ideal for a small size wedding. From garden wedding, backyard wedding, hill wedding to beach wedding, we have plenty of experience in holding an outdoor wedding with a good back- up plan.

Traditional Ceremony

Traditional Ceremony is a very important part of Vietnamese wedding customs. The Planners will help you conceptualize and execute comprehensive decoration for your important days including Ăn Hỏi, Đón Dâu ceremonies at your home.
